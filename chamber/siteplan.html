Restaurant Website Plan Document

Odo's Ghanaian Kitchen

Site Name

Odo's Ghanaian Kitchen

This name was selected because "Odo" means "love" in Twi, one of Ghana's main languages, representing our passion for sharing authentic Ghanaian cuisine. The name immediately communicates our cultural focus while creating a warm, welcoming feeling for our guests.

Optional domain availability: odosghanakitchen.com

Site Purpose

The Odo's Ghanaian Kitchen website serves as the digital gateway to our authentic West African dining experience, providing essential information and facilitating customer engagement. The site will:

  • Showcase our traditional Ghanaian menu with vibrant food photography
  • Enable online reservations and table booking
  • Provide location, hours, and contact information
  • Share our story and cultural heritage
  • Highlight special events, cultural nights, and seasonal offerings
  • Offer online ordering for takeout and delivery
  • Educate visitors about Ghanaian cuisine and dining traditions

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from our customers that the website should address:

  1. First-time Visitor: "What are the must-try authentic Ghanaian dishes at Odo's? I'm unfamiliar with West African cuisine - what would you recommend for someone new to Ghanaian food?"
  2. Ghanaian Expatriate: "Do you serve authentic fufu with light soup on weekends? I'm looking for the real taste of home - how authentic are your recipes?"
  3. Event Planner: "Do you offer catering for cultural events and celebrations? Can you accommodate large groups for traditional Ghanaian naming ceremonies or weddings?"
  4. Dietary Restrictions: "I'm vegetarian - which traditional Ghanaian dishes can be modified to accommodate my dietary needs? Do you have plant-based alternatives to meat stews?"

Color Schema

The color palette is inspired by Ghana's national flag and vibrant cultural aesthetics - representing the rich heritage and energy of Ghanaian culture.

Primary Color
#006B3F

Usage: Headings, navigation elements, footer background, and decorative accents. This rich green represents Ghana's lush forests and agricultural heritage.

Accent Color
#CE1126

Usage: Call-to-action buttons, price tags, special highlights, and interactive elements. This vibrant red symbolizes the blood of ancestors and Ghana's struggle for independence.

Gold Accent
#FFD700

Usage: Special highlights, borders, and premium indicators. Gold represents Ghana's mineral wealth and the bright future ahead.

Text Color
#2C2C2C

Usage: Primary body text for optimal readability against light backgrounds.

Background
#FEF9E7

Usage: Page background color, creating a warm, sunny atmosphere reminiscent of Ghana's climate.

Typography

The typography selection balances cultural authenticity with modern readability, reflecting our restaurant's authentic yet approachable atmosphere.

Headings: Merriweather

Merriweather will be used for all headings (H1, H2, H3) to provide a substantial, traditional feel that complements our authentic culinary experience.

Body Text: Noto Sans

Noto Sans will be used for body text, navigation, menu items, and other content. This clean, versatile sans-serif font offers excellent readability across all devices and languages.
